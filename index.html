About Us

Serving Humanity Through Vallalar’s Teachings of Compassion

  • Our Mission.
  • Our Vision.

Our mission is to spread awareness about the teachings of Ramalinga Vallalar and promote a society built on compassion, equality, and kindness. Through counseling programs, drug awareness initiatives, and educational courses, we aim to guide individuals toward a healthier and more responsible life.

Social Service

Our vision is to build a compassionate and harmonious society inspired by Vallalar’s philosophy of “Jeeva Karunyam” — compassion towards all living beings. We strive to educate communities, support individuals affected by social challenges, and spread Vallalar’s message of humanity through both offline and online learning programs.

Awareness Campaigns

Your donations help us organize public awareness programs, outreach activities, and social support initiatives focused on prevention, recovery, and community safety.

Addiction Counseling

We offer counseling and emotional support for individuals affected by drug addiction, helping them begin their journey toward recovery and rehabilitation.

Police Coordination

We work along with police personnel to support awareness efforts, guide affected individuals, and strengthen community-level action against substance abuse.

Education & Guidance

We conduct educational sessions for students, families, and communities to explain the dangers of drug abuse and the importance of making positive life choices.
