Vallalar History Education & Service Programs

Vallalar Uyir Seva Arakattalai provides educational and awareness programs based on the life and teachings of Ramalinga Vallalar, one of the greatest spiritual reformers who preached compassion, equality, and kindness toward all living beings. His philosophy of “Jeeva Karunyam” teaches humanity to serve others with love and care.

As part of our services, the trust conducts programs that educate people about Vallalar’s life history (Vallalarum Nalvazhvum) , his humanitarian vision, and the importance of living a compassionate life. These sessions help individuals understand the values of unity, selflessness, and social responsibility.

Vallalar Philosophy Courses

Vallalar Uyir Seva Arakattalai offers special courses that explain the life, history, and humanitarian teachings of Ramalinga Vallalar. These programs help individuals understand the values of compassion, equality, and service to all living beings.
